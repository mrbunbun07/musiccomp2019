POCKET OPERATORS

Side Project

Pocket Operators are pocket-sized music creation tools developed by Teenage Engineering. Most of their sounds are synthesized with the exceptions of the models PO-24 and PO-33. They have a built-in sequencer that makes it easy for anyone to create music. They can also be linked together to create a chain of Pocket Operators.

LEARN MORE
Some models, such as the PO-33 KO, can sample sounds using a built-in microphone or a line input.

PO-20 Arcade

Prior to buying a Pocket Operator, I struggled to pick which one to buy. However, I decided on the PO-20 for a few reasons. First, unlike most models, the PO-20 features both drum and melodic elements. Additionally, this models has the 'chord' feature, allowing me to easily write out chord sequences.

Synthesized Arcade Sounds

The Pocket Operator 20 uses many synthesizers to create arcade-like sounds. There are sixteen sounds in total, with two parameters that can be customized to one's preferences. The sounds on the PO-20 are tailored for genres such as chip-tune or EDM.
