KIMDOL

Harper "Kimdol" Kim is a 16 year old music producer and composer based in Vancouver, Canada. He is known for his attention to detail and abstract concepts in his music. During this semester, he has created over 10 songs, worked on various projects, and completed numerous assignments. 

Presentation

Kimdol is coming to Heritage Woods Secondary to do a presentation for the Music Composition and Production class! He will talk about his journey throughout the semester, including the things he has learned and worked on.
